About Me

Hi! I'm Megan, your friendly neighbour photographer!

I have a huge passion for photography and capturing God's amazing creation! I'm here to capture every special moment, making you feel and look amazing while having lots of fun. God is my rock, and I want to capture His beauty for everyone to see! "The heavens declare the glory of God; the skies proclaim the work of his hands." -Psalm 19:1

When I'm not busy with photoshoots or editing you can find me behind a good book, sunset or cloud watching, at church or hanging with the people I love!

I'm here to capture all your Magical Memories!

I'm up for any challenge, whether it's raining or sunny, dressing up or casual, fancy events or relaxing shoots - I'm here for it all - and it will be Magical! You are also more than welcome to bring any props, snacks, outfits etc.

Thank you for trusting me to capture all your precious and magical moments! Being behind my camera brings me great joy, so I will definitely be your biggest cheerleader during your shoot! Also expect a few cheesy jokes!

All my love! x
